Frequently Asked Questions

What is solar energy?

Solar energy is the energy from the sun that is converted into thermal or electrical energy.

What are the benefits of solar energy?

Solar energy reduces electricity bills, is environmentally friendly, and provides energy independence.

How much does solar installation cost in South Africa (in Rands)?

The cost of solar installation in South Africa can range from R80,000 to R150,000 depending on the system size and other factors.

How long does a solar panel system last?

Solar panels typically last between 25 to 30 years with minimal degradation in performance over time.

Will solar panels work during loadshedding?

Yes, solar panels will generate electricity during loadshedding, but you will need a battery storage system to use the electricity when the grid is down.
